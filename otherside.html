The Other Side


While technology has created challenges in the art world,

it has also opened new doors for artists and consumers alike.




The Positive Aspects


Here are some ways in which technology has improved the art world...

OUTREACH


Technology gives art organizations more methods of outreach.

This makes it easier for them to fundraise and engage with their audiences.





DIVERSITY


The Internet is diverse, and different groups of people get to experience art together.

This allows art to evolve, as more people are included in both consumption and creation of art.





ACCESSIBILITY


Physical art supplies deteriorate overtime, especially at faster rates than digital devices.

Digital art is much cheaper in the long run, as most of the tools are virtual.






A Comparison: Digital vs. Traditional Art



DIGITAL TRADITIONAL
Easier to reproduce because of technological features Handmade, therefore hard to reproduce
More accessibility and shareability between digital devices Requires a lot of care, maintenance, and storage after completion to maintain its original form
Flexible because of virtual tools Versatile because of different mediums and materials
